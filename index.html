The Product

Every patient interaction,
handled.

01
Answers every call instantly
No hold music. No voicemail. Every patient greeted within seconds, day or night — 24/7/365.
02
Books directly to your calendar
Checks real-time availability and confirms the appointment on the call — no human step, no callback required.
03
Sends reminders automatically
Outbound reminder calls and SMS at 48 hours and 2 hours before every appointment. Patients confirm, reschedule, or cancel — your calendar updates itself.
04
Triages urgency in real time
Clariva detects urgent language and clinical emergencies — immediately alerting your on-call team or routing to emergency services. It knows exactly when to hand over.
05
Answers every FAQ flawlessly
Hours, directions, insurance accepted, services offered, parking — trained on your specific practice so patients always get the right answer.
06
Gives you complete visibility
Every call logged, transcribed, and summarised. See what patients are asking, when they call, and how many appointments Clariva captured — all in one dashboard.
